Inside the toolbox

A look at what's already shipping.

A non-exhaustive sample of the tools available today across web and mobile — every one of them peer-reviewed against its source guideline.

GIR & Dextrose Mix

Required dextrose %, two-stock mixing instructions, central-line safety bands.

Maintenance Fluids

Holliday-Segar with paediatric tweaks and electrolyte reminders.

Neonatal BP

Gestation-adjusted MAP / SBP percentiles.

BP Percentile

AAP 2017 paediatric BP percentile by age, sex, height.

Bilirubin (≥35 wk)

AAP 2022 hour-specific TSB thresholds, neurotoxicity risk factors, B/A ratio.

Bilirubin (<35 wk)

NICE CG98 graphical thresholds, 23 – 38+ weeks, with intensified-zone logic.

WHO Growth Charts

0 – 5 yr percentiles & z-scores for weight, length/height, head circumference and BMI.

IAP Growth Charts

5 – 18 yr Indian growth references (IAP 2015).

Fenton Preterm

Fenton 2013 preterm growth curves up to 50 weeks.

Schwartz eGFR

Bedside paediatric eGFR with creatinine and height.

Blood Gas Analyser

Acid-base interpretation with compensation checks.

Ventilator Parameters

Tidal volume, MAP, OI / OSI helpers.

Burn Mortality

Lund-Browder body chart, %TBSA and Parkland fluids.

CGA / PMA

Corrected gestational age and post-menstrual age.

NICU Scores

Apgar, SNAPPE-II, CRIB, Downe, Silverman-Anderson.

Drug Formulary

Neofax + Harriet Lane lookup with detail pages.
